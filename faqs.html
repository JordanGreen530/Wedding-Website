Your Questions Answered

What's the dress code?

Garden party chic! Think semi-formal, light, and comfortable for a springtime outdoor setting.

Are kids welcome?

Yes! We’ll have outdoor games and a kid-friendly atmosphere during the ceremony and reception.

Will transportation be provided?

We recommend carpooling or rideshare where possible. There is limited parking at the venue, and we'll update this page if a shuttle is provided.

Can I bring a guest?

If your invitation says "and guest," you’re welcome to bring someone along. If you’re unsure, feel free to reach out via our contact page!
