Cedar Oaks Farm

6766 Goose Creek Valley Rd, Bedford, VA 24523

Located in the scenic foothills between Lynchburg and Roanoke, Cedar Oaks Farm offers a beautiful mountain backdrop and peaceful countryside atmosphere.

Getting There

  • From Roanoke: Take US-460 E toward Bedford (approx. 45 minutes)
  • From Lynchburg: Take US-460 W toward Bedford (approx. 30 minutes)
  • From I-81: Exit onto US-460 E via US-220 or VA-24 depending on your route

Parking

Free parking is available on site. We encourage carpooling due to limited parking spots. If needed, overflow will be coordinated with signage on the day of.
